Nasdaq-100 stocks Optimization

The efficient frontier provides valuable insights for portfolio managers in making informed investment decisions. By using Gurobi (Python), the goal of this project is to calculate optimal weights for the stocks that compose the Nasdaq-100 index to maximize retuns for different risk levels.

Click here for more details
Steps:
1. Importing Data:
List of stocks (Web scraping) and daily stock prices for 10 years from Yahoo Finance (API)
2. Variables Calculation:
Returns, risk, and covariance matrix (NumPy and Pandas)
3. Optimization:
Graphs and analysis about the relationship between the risk level and the return of optimal portfolios (Pyplot) and correlation matrix (Seaborn).
